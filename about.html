About the Game

Eidolon is a pixel game set inside of the Edge Lab of Pisay.

You play as Elijah, a ghost who recently just died inside of the Edge Lab and cannot leave the room. You cannot pass on to the afterlife until you have figured out how you died. The only catch? You have no memory of your death.

Will you be able to figure out your own death or will you have to live the rest of eternity as a wandering inside of the Edge Lab?

Why did you make this game?

I already had this concept a while back, but I never started working on it until 2nd Quarter.

What was your inspiration for making this game?

My intense sense of seeking revenge (jokejokejoke). In all honestly, I just wanted to make a mystery game (WATCH ME IN GR10) and a small room like the CS EDGE Lab was a perfect fit

What were the challenges you faced while making the game?

One was the CSS of every page because when I changed one line of code several others get affected. Another was the time constraints because I started this project later than others

What tools or programs did you use?
